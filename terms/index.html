Use of the App

AutoJournal is an iOS app that automatically generates weekly journal entries from your photo library. Photos are analyzed entirely on-device using Apple's Vision framework. Only anonymized metadata (scene labels, neighborhood-level location names, face counts, time-of-day) is sent to an AI service to generate narrative text. An active subscription is required for full functionality beyond the free tier (4 entries). You agree not to misuse the app or attempt to circumvent its features.

No Account Required

No login or account creation is required. All journal entries and photo data remain stored locally on your device via SwiftData.

Subscriptions & Payments

Third-Party Services

AutoJournal uses a Cloudflare Worker proxy to communicate with Anthropic's Claude API for narrative generation. No photos, GPS coordinates, filenames, or personal identifiers are sent. Only anonymized metadata leaves the device. PostHog is used for anonymous analytics.
